个人简介

毛鹏达，北京航空航天大学可靠飞行控制组博士生，师从全权教授。研究方向为基于参数规划的机器人集群轨迹规划与控制，提出了针对机器人集群运动的最优虚拟管道和正则管道的方法。专著 《多旋翼远程控制实践》 作者之一，目前以第一作者身份在机器人顶级期刊/会议IJRR, RAL, ICRA, IROS上发表多篇论文, 其中IJRR为中国首篇封面论文。 在2021年智在飞翔无人飞行器感知技术竞赛中获得仿真赛废墟场景第一名、实飞赛第二名。 获得中国自动化学会ARTS奖学金、国家留学基金委博士生海外联培基金(CSC)资助在罗兰大学Vicsek教授指导下进行联合培养。曾作为特邀嘉宾参与深蓝学院举办的机器人运动规划系列研讨课讲授。

📚 本人研究
基于多参数规划的MPC方法已应用多年。这种方法称为显式MPC(Explicit MPC)，是一种可预先计算预定义区域内所有可能状态的最优控制量，从而可实时快速做出决策的控制策略。然而，多参数规划在轨迹规划中的应用很少受到关注。因此，我尝试将运筹学中的多参数规划方法应用于轨迹规划，以实现机器人在有限计算约束下的高效稳定的轨迹规划。
主要工作
Tube RRT*

Tube RRT*: Efficient Homotopic Path Planning for Swarm Robotics Passing-Through Large-Scale Obstacle Environments

This paper introduces Tube RRT*, an innovative homotopic path planning method that efficiently generates homotopic paths in large-scale obstacle environments, mitigates swarm congestion, and enhances navigation agility, validated through comprehensive simulations and experiments.

1月 17, 2025
Optimal Virtual Tube

Optimal virtual tube planning and control for swarm robotics

This paper presents a novel method for efficiently solving a trajectory planning problem for swarm robotics in cluttered environments.

4月 23, 2024
Regular Virtual Tube

Making robotics swarm flow more smoothly: a regular virtual tube model

This paper proposes a model of regular virtual tubes to generate safe and smooth paths for drone swarms in obstacle-dense environments, introduces regular principles and conditions, presents a method for obtaining these tubes through trajectory planning, and validates the method's effectiveness and robustness via simulations with random obstacles.

10月 23, 2022
Light Show Platform

Fast light show design platform for k-12 children

This paper introduces a drone swarm light show design platform for K-12 STEAM education, featuring a three-layer architecture (UI, command, and physical layers) that allows children to easily design light shows through an intuitive interface, with actions translated into drone trajectories and supported by a communication router and drone swarm, demonstrating efficiency and applicability in real educational settings.

10月 18, 2021
